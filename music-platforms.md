# Music Platforms

-   **Spotify** — One of the world’s largest streaming services, offering millions of songs, personalized playlists, and algorithm-driven recommendations.
-   **Apple Music** — High-quality music streaming tightly integrated with the Apple ecosystem, offering lossless tracks and curated playlists.
-   **Amazon Music** — Music streaming service with a large catalog, available in tiers (Free/Prime/Unlimited).
-   **YouTube Music** — Music streaming platform based on YouTube’s vast collection, good for songs, remixes, and live versions.
-   **SoundCloud** — A platform focused on independent creators, DJs, remixes, and emerging artists.
-   **Tidal** — Known for high-fidelity audio and exclusive content from certain artists.
