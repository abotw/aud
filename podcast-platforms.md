# Podcast Platforms

-   **Apple Podcasts** — One of the biggest podcast directories, preinstalled on Apple devices, with huge global coverage.
-   **Spotify Podcasts** — A major podcast platform offering exclusive shows and a strong recommendation system.
-   **Google Podcasts (transitioning to YouTube Music)** — A simple, clean podcast app with wide availability.
-   **Pocket Casts** — A popular third-party app with powerful playback controls and cross-device sync.
-   **Overcast** — iOS-only podcast app known for smart speed and voice-boost features.
